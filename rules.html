RULES & GUIDELINES

Participation Guidelines

  • NOESIS is open to undergraduate students enrolled in BE, BCA, BSc (Computer Science/IT), B.Voc, and B.Tech programs within and outside the state of Goa.
  • Teams from outside the state of Goa must compulsorily be accompanied by a faculty member from their institute.
  • Teams must register online and pay a non-refundable registration fee of ₹2000 through online payment.
  • Payment Details:
    Account Name: M/s ROSARY COLLEGE EVENTS EXTRA CURRICULAR ACTIVITIES
    Account Number: 520101031900379
    IFSC Code: UBIN0920835
  • Each team must submit a letter of authorization signed by the Principal of their respective college.
  • All participants must carry their valid college ID cards.
  • A team should consist of 10 students:
    • 8 participants for main events
    • 2 participants for gaming
  • The competition consists of five event categories. Each category requires two students.
  • Participants selecting a category must take part in all sub-events under that category.
  • Once a category is chosen, participants cannot switch to another category.
  • Events under the "Swing of Fortune" category are compulsory and will be included in the grading.

General Rules

  • Code of Conduct: Participants must maintain professionalism and respectful behaviour. Any misconduct will lead to disqualification.
  • Punctuality: Teams must strictly adhere to the event schedule.
  • Dress Code: Participants should wear appropriate formal or casual attire suitable for the event.
  • Personal Belongings: Participants are responsible for their belongings. The organizers are not liable for any loss or damage.
  • Venue Regulations: Maintain cleanliness and avoid damage to property. Any damage caused will be the team’s responsibility.
  • Team Identification: Team ID badges or identification tags must be worn at all times.
  • Fair Play: Cheating, tampering, or rule violations will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Organizer’s Authority: The organizers’ decision will be final and binding in all matters related to the event.
