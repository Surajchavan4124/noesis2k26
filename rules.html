RULES & GUIDELINES

General Rules

  • Code of Conduct: Participants must maintain professionalism and respectful behaviour. Any misconduct will lead to disqualification.
  • Punctuality: Teams must strictly adhere to the event schedule.
  • Dress Code: Participants should wear appropriate formal or casual attire.
  • Personal Belongings: Participants are responsible for their belongings. Organizers are not liable for loss or damage.
  • Venue Regulations: Maintain cleanliness and avoid damage to property.
  • Team Identification: ID badges must be worn at all times.
  • Fair Play: Any cheating or tampering leads to immediate disqualification.
  • Organizer’s Authority: Organizers’ decisions are final and binding.

Participation Guidelines

  • Open to undergraduate students from BE, BCA, BSc (CS/IT), B.Voc, and B.Tech programs.
  • Teams from outside Goa must be accompanied by a faculty member.
  • Mandatory non-refundable registration fee of ₹2000.

  • Five event categories; two students per category.
  • Participants must attend all sub-events of their chosen category.
  • Category switching is not allowed.
  • "Swing of Fortune" events are compulsory and graded.
